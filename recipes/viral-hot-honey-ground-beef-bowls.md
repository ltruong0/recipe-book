# Viral Hot Honey Ground Beef Bowls

With 42 grams of protein and SO much flavor, these Viral Hot Honey Ground Beef Bowls are the best healthy dinner. Make these in 35 minutes!

**Prep Time:** PT5M | **Cook Time:** PT30M | **Total Time:** PT35M | **Servings:** 4

## Ingredients

- 4 1/2 cups cubed sweet potatoes (about 1 1/2 pounds)
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon pepper
- 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 pound lean ground beef
- 1 Tablespoon tomato paste
- 1 Tablespoon taco seasoning
- 2 cups low-fat cottage cheese
- 1 large avocado (or 2 small)
- 4 Tablespoons hot honey

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F.
2. Add the cubed sweet potatoes to a large baking sheet and spray with avocado oil.
3. Add salt, pepper, and cinnamon, then mix to evenly coat.
4. Roast the sweet potatoes for 30 minutes, flipping halfway.
5. Meanwhile, cook the ground beef with the taco seasoning and tomato paste on a large skillet over medium-high heat.
6. Assemble the each bowl with roasted sweet potatoes, cooked ground beef, cottage cheese, avocado slices, and a drizzle of hot honey. Enjoy!
