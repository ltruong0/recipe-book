# Air Fryer Crispy Chicken Caesar

Crispy, golden air-fried chicken breasts coated in a flavorful panko-Parmesan crust, served over fresh romaine lettuce with Caesar dressing and shaved Parmesan. A lighter take on the classic Caesar salad with restaurant-quality results.

**Prep Time:** 10 minutes | **Cook Time:** 15 minutes | **Total Time:** 25 minutes | **Servings:** 2

## Ingredients

- 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
- 1/2 cup panko breadcrumbs
- 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp Italian seasoning
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 1/2 tsp paprika
- 1 egg, beaten
- 1 tbsp olive oil (or spray)
- 1 head romaine lettuce, chopped
- 1/4 cup Caesar dressing
- 1/4 cup shaved Parmesan
- Croutons (optional)
- Lemon wedges (optional)

## Instructions

1. Preheat air fryer to 400°F.
2. Cut chicken breast into thirds and pound thin.
3. Mix panko, Parmesan, garlic powder, Italian seasoning, salt, pepper, and paprika in a bowl.
4. Dip chicken in beaten egg, then coat in the breadcrumb mixture.
5. Lightly spray the air fryer basket with oil and place the chicken inside.
6. Air fry for 12–15 minutes, flipping halfway, until golden and crispy.
7. Toss romaine lettuce with Caesar dressing, shaved Parmesan, and croutons.
8. Add chicken over the salad and squeeze fresh lemon on top for extra flavor.
